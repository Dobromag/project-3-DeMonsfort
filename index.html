Centre for English Language Learning
(English & IELTS course)

DMUK offers a unique approach to English language learning! Not only do incoming students take first year academic English courses, but we also provide our Centre for English Language Learning (CELL) which provides additional english language support for students who require it, but also IELTS test preparation courses for the public! CELL also works in partnership with companies for work-specific English lessons!
